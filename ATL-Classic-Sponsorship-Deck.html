The Opportunity

A Cultural
Powerhouse
Reborn

For 25 years, the Atlanta Football Classic packed 60,000+ fans into the Georgia Dome — making it one of the highest-attended annual HBCU sporting events in the nation.

After a decade-long hiatus following the venue transition, the ATL Classic returns in 2026 with a built-in audience, deep cultural relevance, and the most advanced stadium in the Southeast. This is not a startup event. It's a legacy revival with proven demand, and sponsors who move early will own the narrative of its return.
