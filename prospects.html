Community Health Partner ($50K-$100K)
Grady Health System
Health Partner / $75K
Not a title sponsor candidate, but one of the most narratively powerful partners we can land. Grady is the primary teaching hospital for Morehouse School of Medicine (one of only four historically Black medical schools in America). Sits adjacent to the AUC. Patient population is 70-80% Black/African American. Already the closest Level I trauma center to MBS. The pitch is a Community Health Partner designation: health screenings at the tailgate, sickle cell awareness, "Know Your Numbers" wellness campaign, and a halftime Healthcare Heroes moment. Grady does this work already. We give them the stage.
Ask: $75K cash + $25K-$50K in-kind
